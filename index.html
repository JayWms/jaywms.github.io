Menu Item Descriptions

A plain-language guide to the menu options for each course.

Course 1

Escarole, Honey-Shallot Vinaigrette, Citrus, Candied Walnuts, Goat Cheese

A fresh salad built around escarole, a leafy green with a pleasantly bitter edge. The honey-shallot vinaigrette adds sweet and tangy flavor, while citrus brightens the dish. Candied walnuts bring crunch and sweetness, and goat cheese adds a creamy, slightly tangy finish.

Spinach Arugula Salad, Apples, Date Vinaigrette, Radish, Roasted Pecans, Feta

A mixed green salad with mild spinach and peppery arugula, topped with crisp apples and radish for freshness. The date vinaigrette adds a deeper, slightly caramel-like sweetness. Roasted pecans add crunch, and feta brings a salty, creamy bite.

Roasted Butternut Squash, Burrata, Pepita Salsa Macha

Sweet, tender roasted butternut squash served with burrata, a rich and creamy fresh cheese. Pepita salsa macha is a chili oil-style topping with pumpkin seeds that adds crunch, nuttiness, and a little heat.

Roasted Carrots, Citrus Crema, Hot Honey, Toasted Pine Nuts

Roasted carrots with concentrated natural sweetness, balanced by a tangy citrus cream. Hot honey adds a sweet-spicy glaze, and toasted pine nuts bring a buttery crunch.

Course 2

Farfalle, Roasted Kale, Preserved Lemon Butter, Seasonal Mushrooms

Bowtie pasta tossed with earthy seasonal mushrooms and roasted kale. The preserved lemon butter sauce is rich and silky with a bright, slightly salty citrus flavor.

Cavatelli, Beurre Monté, Yuzu Kosho, Breadcrumbs, Nappa Cabbage

Cavatelli pasta in a smooth butter-based sauce known as beurre monté. Yuzu kosho, a Japanese citrus-chili paste, adds sharp citrus flavor and gentle heat. Napa cabbage softens into the dish, while breadcrumbs add a crisp finish.

Orecchiette, Guajillo Chile, Sun Dried Tomatoes, Basil, Pine Nuts

Orecchiette pasta with a savory sauce featuring guajillo chile, which is mildly spicy and slightly smoky. Sun-dried tomatoes add concentrated sweetness and tang, basil brings freshness, and pine nuts add texture and nuttiness.

Shiitake Mushroom Risotto, Grana Padano, Seasonal Herbs

A creamy risotto made by slowly cooking rice until rich and velvety. Shiitake mushrooms give it a deep, earthy flavor, Grana Padano adds a nutty salty cheese note, and seasonal herbs brighten the finish.

Course 3

Pork Chop, Agrodolce, Roasted Broccolini, Farro

A hearty pork chop served with agrodolce, an Italian sweet-and-sour sauce that balances richness with acidity. Roasted broccolini adds a lightly charred green vegetable element, and farro contributes a chewy, nutty grain base.

Seared Salmon, Creamy Leeks, Shaved Brussels Sprouts, XO Sauce

Seared salmon paired with soft, creamy leeks and thinly sliced Brussels sprouts for freshness and crunch. XO sauce adds a bold, savory, umami-rich flavor with a slightly seafood-forward profile.

Chicken Breast, Garlic Confit Mashed Potatoes, Braised Collard Greens, Mushroom Butter Sauce

A classic comfort-style entrée with chicken breast, ultra-smooth mashed potatoes enriched with mellow slow-cooked garlic, tender braised collard greens, and a rich mushroom butter sauce.

Course 4

Pear Tart, Cardamom Coconut Ice Cream

A fruit tart with sweet pear in a buttery crust, served with coconut ice cream flavored with cardamom, which adds a warm, aromatic spice note.

Olive Oil Cake, Orange Marmalade, Brown Butter Whipped Cream

A moist, tender olive oil cake with a subtle fruity richness. Orange marmalade adds bright citrus and slight bitterness, while brown butter whipped cream brings a nutty, caramel-like flavor.

Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream, Cookie Crumble, Glazed Strawberries

A creamy dessert with the flavor of strawberry cheesecake in ice cream form, topped with crunchy cookie crumble and glossy sweet strawberries.
